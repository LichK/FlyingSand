Flying-Sand Publisher

Flying-Sand Publisher is indeed a deployment system. Its target is to be trackable, flexible, scalable and easy to use.

Trackable

Settings are all tracked by git.

Flexible

Settings are clojure data and functions.

Scalable

It can handle a lots of slaves with only a little increasement of memory.

Easy to Use

It can be communicated by HTTP. That is to say, anything, including scripts, can talk with it.
